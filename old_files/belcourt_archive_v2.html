About this archive

What's not included

Things not mentioned here: Rental Screenings, Seminars, Film Festivals not associated with the Belcourt (NJFF, BANFF, REEL ROCK).

Series

The series column is still being updated. We added some of the bigger series the Belcourt has done, but we are certainly missing a lot of them.

35mm prints

In 2019, internal Belcourt document logs started noting when we played 35mm prints of films. This has not been added to every instance that a 35mm print was shown of a film. However, if the logs say we played a print of the film, I have added a note to the entry.

Catch a mistake?

Submit it! We'll fix it. Submit the title of the film, the date/year it played, and what the mistake is.
