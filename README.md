# Belcourt Theatre Film Archive

A complete record of every film screened at Nashville's Belcourt Theatre across its 100+ year history.

## About

The Belcourt Theatre Film Archive is a history project dedicated to documenting every film that has ever played at The Belcourt Theatre in Nashville, Tennessee. From its earliest days to the present, this archive serves as a living record of one of Nashville's most beloved cinemas — a place with over a century of film history.

## About the Belcourt

The Belcourt Theatre is a historic cinema in Nashville, Tennessee, with a history spanning more than 100 years. It remains an active, beloved cultural institution in the city, known for its independent and repertory programming.

## Purpose

This archive exists for anyone curious about the Belcourt's cinematic history — whether you're a film lover, a local historian, or just want to know if your favorite movie ever played there. It is designed for casual browsing.

## Author
